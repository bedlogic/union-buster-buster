Bevy of Bots

Union Buster Buster was made in two days for GAMERella 2014: "Boss Up" at NYU Game Center. We wrote the game in Unity. We created the sprites in Pixen. We wrote the music on Gameboy using LSDJ. We wrote the website in Sass and html. Inspired by Teachers and Students for a Better Kaplan and Rude Mechanical Orchestra.
